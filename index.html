PT Sae Agro Pinanjar

Our company has Products natural sugar made from coconut palm sap, which is the sugary circulating fluid of the coconut plant. The deep understanding about the people and culture in this region coupled with international organic certification standards position us as the leading supplier of organic coconut sugar and nectar.

Our Company 'From Our Local Manufacture with Quality'

PT Sae Agro Pinanjar is a company that connect and strengthens local Indonesian craftsmen and farmers to the world wide trading. We are committed being the best partner of local artisans and farmers to compete in world market and traders. We supply and intensively export Indonesian handicrafts and farmers' products to 5 continents. Our Vision is to become the best and trusted supplier of agricultural commodities and contribute in local community's economy improvement. To reach our vision, we do collaboration with producers and farmers to create and provide quality products for global market and customers. Currently, we produce Powder Coconut Sugar, natively known as Gula Semut, but people prefer call it Palm Sugar.
