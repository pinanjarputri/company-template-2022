SAE AGRO PINANJAR

PT. Sae Agro Pinanjar is a leading Trader of from Indonesia. Our modern factory is situated in Kendari in South East Sulawesi, a prominent coconut growing region in Indonesia. We use only carefully selected, fresh, mature and high grade coconuts to manufacture our desiccated coconut. We produce high fat desiccated coconut suitable for bakery and confectionery goods as well as any other forms of cuisine. Our desiccated coconut has a pleasant coconut taste and aroma, and features a clean white color free from color or material impurities.
